About Me

Hello! I'm Sayali

I'm a self-taught artist based in Mumbai, passionate about creating beautiful resin art pieces that bring joy and uniqueness to your spaces.

My journey into the world of resin art started with curiosity and experimentation. Today, I create stunning resin art pieces, charcoal art, canvas paintings, and love exploring various crafts and DIY projects.

What drives me is the opportunity to teach and share my knowledge with beginners. Through my workshops, I help aspiring artists discover the magic of resin art in the easiest and most fun way possible.
